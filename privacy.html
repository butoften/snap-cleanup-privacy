"Snap Cleanup" Privacy Policy

Effective date: July 5, 2026

Contact: service@butof.com

English

"Snap Cleanup" respects your privacy. This policy explains how the "Snap Cleanup" mobile app handles information when you use it to find, review, and delete screenshots.

Information We Do Not Collect

The app itself does not collect, upload, sell, or share your photos or personal data. It does not include account registration, advertising, third-party analytics, or tracking SDKs.

Photo Library Access

"Snap Cleanup" requests access to your photo library only so it can identify screenshots, show them for your review, and delete selected screenshots after your confirmation. Photo processing happens locally on your device. Your photos are not uploaded to our servers.

If you grant limited photo access, the app can only work with the photos you selected. You can change or revoke photo permissions at any time in iOS Settings.

Local App Data

The app may store your language preference locally on your device. This setting is not uploaded to us.

Contact by Email

If you contact us by email, we may receive your email address and the content of your message. We use this information only to respond to your support request.

Data Retention and Deletion

Because the app does not upload your photos or app usage data to our servers, there is no server-side photo data for us to delete. You can remove local app data by deleting the app from your device. Screenshots deleted through the app are handled by the operating system and may be moved to Recently Deleted or Trash, depending on the platform.

Children's Privacy

"Snap Cleanup" is not designed to collect personal information from children. The app does not knowingly collect personal information from anyone.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. If we make changes, we will update the effective date on this page.

Contact Us

If you have questions about this privacy policy, contact us at service@butof.com.

中文

"清截"尊重你的隐私。本政策说明"清截" App 在帮助你查找、预览和删除截图时如何处理信息。

我们不会收集的信息

App 本身不会收集、上传、出售或共享你的照片或个人数据。App 不包含账号注册、广告、第三方分析或跟踪 SDK。

相册访问

"清截"仅在你授权后访问相册，用于识别截图、展示给你预览，并在你确认后删除选中的截图。照片处理过程在你的设备本地完成，不会上传到我们的服务器。

如果你授予的是受限相册访问权限，App 只能处理你选择的照片。你可以随时在 iOS 系统设置中修改或撤销相册权限。

本地应用数据

App 可能会在设备本地保存你的语言偏好设置。该设置不会上传给我们。

通过邮件联系我们

如果你主动通过邮件联系我们，我们可能会收到你的邮箱地址和邮件内容。我们仅将这些信息用于回复你的支持请求。

数据保留与删除

由于 App 不会把你的照片或使用数据上传到我们的服务器，因此我们没有服务器端照片数据需要删除。你可以通过删除 App 来移除本地应用数据。通过 App 删除的截图由操作系统处理，可能会被移动到“最近删除”或回收站，具体取决于平台。

儿童隐私

"清截"并非为了收集儿童个人信息而设计。App 不会有意收集任何人的个人信息。

政策更新

我们可能会不时更新本隐私政策。如有更新，我们会修改本页面的生效日期。

联系我们

如对本隐私政策有任何问题，请通过 service@butof.com 联系我们。
