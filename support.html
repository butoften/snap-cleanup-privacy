English

If you need help with "Snap Cleanup", want to report a problem, or have feedback, please contact us by email.

Email

service@butof.com

What to Include

  • Your device model and iOS version
  • The app version you are using
  • A short description of the issue or feedback
  • Screenshots of the issue, if available

About Photo Access

"Snap Cleanup" uses system photo permissions to find and review screenshots locally on your device. If screenshot search does not show all photos, please check whether photo access is set to Limited Access or Full Access in iOS Settings.

关于相册权限

"清截"使用系统相册权限在设备本地查找和预览截图。如果搜索截图时没有显示全部照片，请在 iOS 系统设置中检查相册权限是否为“受限访问”或“完全访问”。
